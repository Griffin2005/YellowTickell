About YellowTickell

Heritage craft, modern technology, and responsible fashion.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the textile industry, YellowTickell Pvt. Ltd. has been at the forefront of innovation in fabric manufacturing. We started our journey with a simple mission: to create textiles that not only meet the highest quality standards but also contribute to a sustainable future.

Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted partner for fashion designers, retailers, and textile enthusiasts worldwide. We believe that great fabrics are the foundation of great fashion, and we're dedicated to providing our clients with materials that inspire creativity and stand the test of time.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly dyes and closed-loop water systems to minimize environmental impact while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Innovation

Continuous R&D investment helps us develop better fabrics, finishes, and manufacturing processes that set industry standards.

Quality

Rigorous testing and quality control ensure that every piece of fabric meets our exacting standards for durability and comfort.
